Ih this plot we compare the amount of people leaving or entering the desease

In every day we have 3 quantities: the one above the \(0\) in the \(y\) axis is the amount of positives found in this day. Below the \(0\) we find the deceased \(+\) the healed. The up-stack is the stream of people incoming into the bucket of the infected; the down-stack is the stream of people leaving the bucket of the infected.

When the up-stack is bigger than the the down-stack, the overall infected are decreasing.

The above shaded plot is the difference of the two stacks i.e. the number of actual infected people. When the plot falls below \(0\), the infected are decreasing.
