In this plot we consider the number of tests

\(positives \over tests\) is the ratio between the overall number of infected and the overall number of tests. This plot is also approximated with a Gaussian fit.

\(daypositives \over \Delta(tests)\) is the daily rate of infected over tests. This is the more important variable to evaluate the spreading of the desease.

\(\Delta(positives) \over \Delta(tests)\) is the ratio between the difference of infected and tests. On the other hand, this variable is quite misleading.
