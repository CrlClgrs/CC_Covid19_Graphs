In this plot we show the real and forecasted behaviour of the main variables

Every variable in this plot is shown against its forecasted value. Forecasts are obtained mocking the differential S-I-R model that rules this kind of phenomena.

In NEW_VALUES_ON_TOT_POSITIVI we calculated the ratio between the daily value and the total number of positives in day. Those curves are approximated by means of a polynomial fit and that approximation is used here to calculate the est curves. The more suitable order of the polynomial approximation is chosen by visual inspection.

The forecasts differ from the ones in ABSOLUTE_STACKS due to the different kind of approximation.
