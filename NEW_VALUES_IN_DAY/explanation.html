In this plot we summarize the main daily values

Daily values need a bit of attention: the positives of day is the number of infected people in this day. This is not the difference between the infected of today and the ones of yesterday because this quantity is cleared of healed and deceased (as we can see in NEW_VALUES_STACKS).

Here we show the positives of the day stacked with healed and deceased: this is the total number of cases of the single day. In fact, every day the total cases are the sum of people just infected plus the ones who left the desease since healed or deceased.
