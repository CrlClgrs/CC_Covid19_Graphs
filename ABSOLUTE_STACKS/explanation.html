In this plot we show some overall values

The stacks of the overall cases shows how many people have been in touch with the virus (positives+healed+deceased). This curve will flatten as long as the time goes on meaning that no new cases will be added to the stack. Its behaviour is approximated and forecasted with the use of a Logistic curve. The logistic curve is calculated as integration of a Gaussian fitting the differential data (i.e. the difference of every day from the the day before). The same forecasting technique is used for the deceased curve.
