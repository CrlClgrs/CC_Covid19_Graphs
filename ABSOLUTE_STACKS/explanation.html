In this plot we show the overall values

The stacks of the cases shows how many people have been in touch with the virus until the current day \((\)positives\(+\)healed\(+\)deceased\()\). This curve will flatten as time goes by, meaning that no new cases will be added to the stack. Its behaviour is approximated and forecasted with the use of a Logistic curve. The Logistic curve is calculated as integration of a Gaussian fitting the differential data (i.e. the difference of every day from the the day before). The same forecasting technique is used for the deceased curve.

The positives clearly have a different behaviour: the curve goes like a a Gaussian itself since the infected will point to \(0\).
